How We Review Gear

At The Bad Guitarist, every review is based on real playing experience rather than first impressions. We spend time using the gear during practice, recording, and everyday playing before sharing our opinions. Our goal is to help musicians make informed decisions by discussing both the strengths and weaknesses of every product.

We do not believe in perfect gear, only gear that inspires you to play. Whether a piece of equipment costs $50 or $5,000, our goal is to help you understand what it does well, where it falls short, and whether it is the right fit for your style.
